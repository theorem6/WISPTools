# WispTools v1.0.0 – First release

First public release of **WISP Multitool (WispTools)** – the complete wireless ISP management platform.

## Highlights

- **Multi-tenant** – Organization isolation, roles, tenant switching
- **HSS & subscribers** – Open5GS HSS, S6a/Diameter, bandwidth plans, bulk import
- **CBRS** – Google SAS & Federated Wireless integration
- **ACS/TR-069** – CPE provisioning, firmware, monitoring
- **PCI planning** – LTE conflict resolution and optimization
- **Customer portal** – White-label branding, billing, tickets
- **SvelteKit** frontend, **Firebase** (Auth, Hosting, Functions), **Node on GCE** backend, **MongoDB Atlas**
